My Projects

Student-Hub WebSite

BtechNews is a specialized mobile application tailored to the needs of students pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (Btech) degree. This app serves as a comprehensive platform designed to empower Btech students throughout their academic journey. The app provides a curated stream of news articles, research breakthroughs, and advancements in technology and engineering, ensuring students stay updated on the latest developments in their field. It offers academic support by providing study guides, sample papers, and resources that enhance students’ understanding of complex concepts within the Btech curriculum. “Career Pathways” offers insights into potential career options, industry trends, and internship opportunities, accompanied by advice from successful Btech alumni. The “Project Showcase” feature enables students to share and explore innovative projects, fostering creativity and collaboration within the Btech community. Students can discover upcoming tech events, workshops, webinars, and conferences relevant to their field of study, enhancing their networking and learning opportunities. The app includes study groups and forums where Btech students can connect, discuss coursework, share study materials, and collaborate on projects, promoting peer learning and mutual support. A “Resource Library” provides access to digital textbooks, research papers, and reference materials, facilitating comprehensive and efficient learning. “Industry Insights” offer real-world applications of engineering principles through case studies, success stories, and practical examples, bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry practices. Time management tools assist students in effectively organizing their schedules and academic tasks, promoting efficient time utilization. The app features an “Expert Q&A” section where students can seek advice and solutions from experienced professionals and professors in the technology and engineering fields. “BtechNews” is a holistic companion that supports Btech students not only in their academic pursuits but also in their personal and professional growth within the ever-evolving landscape of technology and engineering.
